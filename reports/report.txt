Problem Statement

A multi-speciality hospital chain has experienced a 25% increase in patient complaints related to long waiting times and appointment delays. Hospital leadership requires an analytics solution to monitor patient flow, evaluate department performance, improve resource utilization, and enhance patient satisfaction. The objective is to build an interactive dashboard that provides operational insights and supports strategic decision-making.

Objectives

Analyze hospital operational data.
Measure patient waiting time and appointment delays.
Monitor bed utilization and hospital admissions.
Evaluate department and doctor performance.
Analyze patient satisfaction trends.
Track revenue generated by hospital services.
Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for hospital management.

Data Preprocessing

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

Loaded the dataset using Pandas.
Checked dataset dimensions and data types.
Handled missing values using suitable replacement techniques.
Removed duplicate records.
Converted date and time columns into appropriate formats.
Created derived features such as Appointment Delay, Bed Utilization, Department Load, and Waiting Category.
Verified data consistency before analysis.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes the following healthcare KPIs:

Total Patients
Average Waiting Time
Bed Utilization Rate
Patient Satisfaction Score
Appointment Delay Percentage
Emergency Cases
Department Performance
Revenue Generated

These KPIs provide a comprehensive overview of hospital operational efficiency.

Dashboard Development
Executive Overview

The Executive Overview page displays high-level KPIs including Total Patients, Revenue Generated, Average Satisfaction, Average Waiting Time, Appointment Delay Percentage, and Bed Utilization Rate. Trend charts show patient visits over time, while department-wise revenue and emergency case distribution provide management with a quick operational overview.

Patient Flow Dashboard

This dashboard analyzes patient movement within the hospital. It includes patient admissions, department activity, peak-hour patient load, and appointment trends. These visualizations help identify departments experiencing high patient traffic and periods of increased demand.

Operational Dashboard

The Operational Dashboard focuses on hospital efficiency by monitoring doctor utilization, appointment delays, bed occupancy, and department performance. Managers can identify operational bottlenecks and optimize resource allocation.

Patient Experience Dashboard

This dashboard evaluates patient satisfaction trends, analyzes the relationship between waiting time and satisfaction, and measures service quality across departments. It helps hospital administrators identify factors affecting the patient experience.

Recommendations

Improve appointment scheduling to reduce delays.
Increase staff allocation in high-demand departments.
Monitor bed utilization regularly.
Optimize doctor schedules during peak hours.
Continuously track patient satisfaction.
Implement real-time monitoring using Power BI dashboards.
Review operational KPIs regularly to support strategic planning.
