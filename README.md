# Smart Hospital Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Problem Statement

A multi-speciality hospital chain has experienced a significant increase in patient complaints regarding long waiting times and appointment delays. Hospital management requires a data-driven analytics solution to monitor patient flow, optimize resource utilization, improve operational efficiency, and enhance patient satisfaction. The objective of this project is to build a Smart Hospital Analytics Dashboard that provides actionable insights into hospital operations and supports informed decision-making.

---

## 2. Dataset Description

## 3. KPI Definitions

**Total Patients** – Total number of unique patients who visited the hospital.

**Average Waiting Time** – Average time patients waited before consultation.

**Bed Utilization Rate** – Percentage of occupied beds based on patient admissions.

**Patient Satisfaction Score** – Average satisfaction rating provided by patients.

**Appointment Delay %** – Percentage of appointments delayed beyond the scheduled time.

**Emergency Cases** – Total number of patients classified as emergency cases.

**Department Performance** – Evaluation of each department based on patient volume, waiting time, satisfaction score, and revenue.

**Revenue Generated** – Total revenue collected from patient bills.

## 5. Key Findings

* Departments with higher patient volumes experienced longer waiting times and greater operational pressure.
* Long waiting times were associated with lower patient satisfaction scores.
* Appointment delays increased during peak consultation hours.
* Bed utilization remained higher when patient admissions increased, indicating higher demand for inpatient services.
* Emergency cases contributed to consultation delays by receiving priority treatment.
* Revenue generation varied across departments depending on patient volume and services provided.
* Monitoring operational KPIs enabled quick identification of bottlenecks and areas requiring improvement.

---

## 6. Recommendations

* Improve appointment scheduling to reduce patient waiting times.
* Increase staffing levels in departments with consistently high patient loads.
* Allocate additional resources during peak hospital hours.
* Continuously monitor bed occupancy to optimize inpatient management.
* Reduce appointment delays by balancing doctor workloads.
* Regularly track patient satisfaction and service quality metrics.
* Use real-time dashboards to support operational planning and decision-making.

---

## 7. Future Scope

Future enhancements can include integrating real-time hospital information systems, predictive analytics for patient admissions, machine learning models to forecast waiting times, automated staff scheduling, and AI-based resource allocation. The dashboard can also be expanded with patient readmission analysis, disease trend monitoring, and mobile reporting capabilities to support smarter healthcare management.
